# Granola

## Ingredients
- 300g oats
- 30g coconut flakes
- 100g hemp seeds
- 100g dark syrup

## Instructions
- Pre-heat oven to 150 degrees celcius
- Mix everything up in a bowl
- Spread on a baking sheet on a baking tray
- Bake for 20 min at 150C
- Turn down the heat to 120C and bake for another 30 min
