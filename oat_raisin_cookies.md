# Oat Raisin Cookies
Crispy and tasty. Good for long runs ;)

## Ingredients
- 5 dl rolled oats
- 0.5 tsp baking powder
- 65 g dark syrup
- 1 dl hemp seeds
- 0.5 dl coconut flakes
- 2 dl oat milk
- 75 g raisins
- 1 tsp cardamom
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions
- Mix all ingredients together well
- Use a spoon to make the dough into ~20 cookies
- Bake in oven for 30 min at 175C
